About Us

Headquartered in Beijing Zhangjiang High-Tech Development Zone, Beijing Saints Sages Surgical Co., Ltd. was founded in December 2017.  Being a high-tech enterprise specialising in the research and development, production and sales of premium energy surgical products, the company has a core team composed of domestic senior personnel and global industry elites.

 

 Today, the company possesses its layout of several energy surgical products in the field of minimally invasive surgery with complete core intellectual property rights.  Some of the products are the first in the world.  In the near future, the company will continue to focus on its specialism with high technical barrier of entry, further enriching its portfolio.

 

The company firmly adheres to clinical needs to achieve quality assurance, innovation and leadership, and becomes the most trusted partner of healthcare professionals
