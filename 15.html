SanAgile II Features
Loaded with RTTE II (Real Time Tissue Effect 2nd Generation) algorithm, this upgrade pre-monitors tissue volume and optimizes energy parameters, maximizing intraoperative smoothness.
The scalpel hosts a built-in algorithm chip, which has independent and unique operation calibration.
Without a conventional transducer, SanAgile II does not require recurrent sterilization or preoperative assembly, which eases the end-users’ procurement pressure.
The grip is ergonomically designed.
SanAgile II Details

The curved jaw design of the shear is beneficial to wider observation fields. The tip is coated with anti-slip PTFE materials to facilitate fine dissection.

The shaft has 2 optional lengths: 23cm and 36cm respectively.
