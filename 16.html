SanAgile I  Features
The mainframe is loaded with RTTE I (Real Time Tissue Effect 1st Generation) algorithm. The algorithm enables real-time energy react in response to the tissue effect throughout the whole process for better tissue cutting speed and blood coagulation. Before use, neither gear modulation nor scalpel self-check is needed.
The transducer is equipped with high-performance piezoelectric ceramics to ensure efficient energy conversion and intraoperative smoothness.
The shear has a curved jaw design, which facilitates observation of the operation filed; its torque wrench and rotary wheel are combined for convenience; and the grip is ergonomically designed.
SanAgile I  Details

The curved jaw design of the shear is beneficial to wider observation fields and fine dissection.

The shaft has 3 optional lengths: 14cm, 23cm and 36cm respectively.
